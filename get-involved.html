Get Involved

To join OEDSI, read a description of these roles and fill out your information on the form below.

Description of Roles ~


School Administrators:

  • Upload school or school district raw data you would like analyzed.
  • We will work with you to define the research question(s) and parameters of data analysis.
  • Data must be delivered in a certain format to minimize the time required for data analysis and maximize its predictive power.
  • All data uploaded and analyzed, will be consolidated as data points into our big data algorithms to provide meta-analysis predictive power to a public audience.

Benefits:

  • Data analysis will be free!
  • Receive actionable insights that address mission-critical organizational objectives you define.
  • Save valuable time and money that can be used elsewhere.
  • If approved by our Program Manager, your data analysis will be delivered to you within 1 month and a publication manuscript draft will be prepared.
  • Learn about data and outcomes from schools through monthly publications and annual reports.

Data Analysts:

  • Will work to acquire and consolidate publically available school data ie. SARC reports and other sources of information.
  • Will clean data and make sure it is in the optimal machine-readable format for interoperability.
  • Based on your familiarity with Data Science, you can apply the techniques you learn to socially-meaningful projects.

Benefits:

  • We will provide you with resources to learn Data Science, through your own diligent study. We will soon create professional learning communities to teach ourselves through tutorials, open hours and other teaching activities we host to help each other.
  • Access the large database you are helping build, to power projects you propose.
  • Leadership opportunities exist for you to play a large role in our volunteer network - everything from technology, marketing, multimedia, project management and other roles.
  • Document your deliverables in a portfolio through our publications, Google Colab Notebooks, GitHub, Kaggle and more to increase professional development and job opportunities.

Researchers:

  • Upload school data or your manuscript data results you would like analyzed.
  • You may serve as an Editor of our open access dataset and annotate results.

Benefits:

  • We will provide materials to help you learn the fundamentals of Data Science.
  • Contribute to an ongoing collection of research projects, which tackle a host of educational issues.
  • Publication opportunities are abundant, through oedsi.org and numerous Op-Eds we intend to submit each month to numerous outlets.

Software Engineers:

  • Based on your skillset, building data analysis tools through collaborative notebooks.
  • Developing scripts and automation tools to acquire data in optimal formats, to save time for Data Analysts and Educators.
  • Can be provided additional permissions such as web development management opportunities and to develop technologies to solve educational problems in the field.

Benefits:

  • We will provide you with resources to learn Data Science, through your own diligent study.
  • Access the large database you are helping build, to power projects you propose.
  • Document your deliverables in a portfolio through our publications, Google Colab Notebooks, GitHub, Kaggle and more to increase professional development and job opportunities.
  • Leadership opportunities exist for you to play a large role in our volunteer network - everything from technology, marketing, multimedia, project management and other roles.
