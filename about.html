About Us

The Open Educational Data Science Initiative (OEDSI) is an open source initiative committed to acquiring, cleaning and understanding metrics on school success and educational policy; with the goal of using Data Science and Machine Learning to present data that can assist educators, school administrators and policymakers. Our goal is to present actionable information that can be piloted in school environments to boost educational outcomes.

We crowdsource and consolidate data into open source datasets that enable fully-transparent data analyses. We present new information curated monthly publications which include rich visual data, interactive notebooks and a summary statement of new findings. We also publish manuscripts and Op-Eds in a variety of venues to promote dissemination of our results.

As a grassroots citizen science initiative, anyone can join as a contributor to oedsi.org. Visit our Get Involved page to learn more.

Technology ~

We use technology to:

  • Connect a community of educators and technologists to plan and contribute to projects across the world.
  • Develop automated scripts and web spiders to consolidate publicly available data on United States high schools (for now).
  • Simplify big data and present trends that are too messy, scattered and difficult to see with the naked human eye.
  • Built predictive models (regression) to predict the outcomes of educational interventions to school and school district metrics.
  • Share interactive data analyses through Google Colab, Tableau and other tools. Participants can clearly see how the data was processed and adapt it to their projects.
  • Teach data science, computer science and assessment literacy to a lay audience.
  • Provide an open access publication medium to submit and disseminate educational findings.
